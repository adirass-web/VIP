## Panel's recommended shortlist

1. **Seter** — the brand essence (tailored + secret shelter) in one Hebrew word that travels.
2. **Ancile** — the secrecy-as-method story; most "Q" of the classical set.
3. **Armiger** — the white-glove *service* relationship, not a product.
4. **Sub Rosa** — if discretion should lead over protection.
5. **Metzuda** — if Israeli weight should lead.
