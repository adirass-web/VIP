## The organizing principle — two axes

Everything sorts on two questions:

1. **What triggers the buyer?**
   - **Dispute (reactive):** a known adversary, a relationship gone hostile. *Current product.*
   - **Precautionary (proactive):** a planned event raises exposure before anything has gone wrong.
   - **Post-incident (acute):** something already happened; the phone is ringing now.

2. **Does it fit the existing system** — the "nobody had to hack anything" wedge, the trusted-access vignette template, the *one day / in person / no app* promise — **or does it need new design?**

The five live pages are all **Dispute + fits-the-system**. That box is nearly full. Growth means deciding how far to step outside it, and accepting the design cost when you do.

## Category B — Precautionary / life-event (the NEW category)

Proactive, calendar-driven, calmer buyer, often repeat/retainer. Travel is the flagship. **These do not fit the current wedge** — there's no betrayer, no "door left open"; the threat is environmental or status-driven. They need a **second template** and a small positioning widening from *"resolution of a dispute"* to *"protection at a moment of raised exposure."*

| Situation | Buyer | Channel | Why it breaks the wedge | **Decision (rec.)** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Travel to a risky country / clean devices** | Exec, dealmaker, lawyer, journalist, researcher | Corporate security, family office, cross-border law | At a hostile border/hotel, things *are* seized & implanted — "nobody hacked anything" inverts | **NEXT (flagship of new category)** |
| **Taking a public role / sudden visibility** | New appointee, board member, pre-IPO founder, someone newly in the news | Comms advisors, boards, VCs | You become a target by *status*, not by a relationship | **LATER** |
| **Wealth / liquidity event** | Just sold a company; received a large inheritance | Wealth managers, private bankers, lawyers | Exposure follows the money (fraud, extortion, doxxing) — no adversary yet | **LATER** |
| **Relocation / aliyah / emigration** | Person rebuilding a digital life in a new country | Relocation advisors, immigration lawyers | Setup task, not a defence against anyone | **LATER** |
| **Family-office / principal-and-family retainer** | UHNW family | Family office, private bankers | Ongoing posture, not a one-day event | **LATER (but best recurring revenue)** |

**Strategic read:** opening this category is the single biggest growth lever — calmer buyers, better margins, repeat/retainer revenue, and channels you don't touch today (wealth, corporate security, family offices). The cost is one new template + careful promise reconciliation. Recommendation: **prove the category with Travel first**, learn, then decide whether to add the rest.

## Cross-cutting implications (what expansion actually costs)

1. **A second page template.** Category B can't reuse the betrayal-vignette structure. It needs its own spine: *name the moment → make the environmental threat concrete → show the finite, calm protocol → certificate/return.* One template serves all of B.
2. **Promise-line reconciliation.** *"No app — nothing of ours lives on your device"* collides with a **loaner device that is ours and travels with the client.** Fix in copy: the loaner is explicitly *ours, issued and reclaimed* — the principle (*nothing of ours stays on **your** device, ever*) holds because the loaner replaces, not infects, the personal device. Worth a one-line canonical statement before drafting.
3. **Engagement shape.** Disputes are *one day, in person.* Travel is **two touchpoints (pre-trip provision + post-trip sanitization).** The "single day" promise needs a travel-specific phrasing.
4. **Pricing / SKUs.** Category B likely wants its own SKU(s) — a per-trip price and/or a frequent-traveler retainer — distinct from the dispute tiers (Personal ₪15,000 … Sovereign ₪95,000). Single price only, your call. (Retainer precedent already exists: Priority Retainer ₪12,000/yr.)
5. **New referral channels.** Wealth managers, family offices, corporate security, cross-border law — none of which the current attorney-centric funnel addresses.
6. **Honesty ceiling (Category B-1).** You cannot promise to defeat a customs officer demanding an unlock or a state implant. The defensible, on-voice claim is *"you carry nothing worth taking."* Scoping integrity matters more here than on any dispute page.

## The travel offer — loaner-hardware model (your chosen direction)

**One-line offer:** *Before you fly into a hostile environment, we hand you a clean device that holds nothing worth taking — and when you land back, we make sure nothing followed you home.*

**Engagement shape (two touchpoints + the trip):**
1. **Pre-trip (in person):** profile the trip and threat environment; issue a stripped loaner phone (and laptop if needed) with only what the trip requires, minimal accounts, hardened comms (Signal), eSIM/connectivity plan; brief the client on borders, hotel devices, networks, and a duress/compliance posture. Personal devices stay home, untouched.
2. **The trip:** client carries only the loaner. Nothing personal to seize, image, or implant.
3. **Post-trip (in person):** reclaim and forensically sanitize/re-image the loaner; verify the client's home devices and accounts are clean of anything introduced; confirm safe re-entry to normal digital life.

**What's new vs. the dispute model:**
- **Inventory / capex:** a small fleet of loaner devices + a re-imaging/sanitization workflow. (Operational decision — sourcing, quantity, refresh cadence.)
- **Two visits, not one** — phrasing for the promise.
- **A repeat buyer:** frequent travelers → natural retainer.

**Template needs:** the Category-B spine above; threat made concrete via *environment* (border imaging, evil-maid, hostile networks) not betrayal; proof via *protocol + standards*, not restoried vignettes (no betrayed-spouse story fits here — possibly a sourced, real-world border-seizure reference instead).

**Promise line (proposed, for your approval):** *In person, both ends · A clean device that's ours, not yours · No cloud · No logs · Nothing of ours stays behind.*

**Pricing placement:** new SKU — single price, TBD (your call). Candidates: a per-trip *Clean-Device Detail* and/or a *Frequent-Traveler* retainer. Not mapped to the dispute tiers.

**State-of-the-art to pull before building (flagged, not yet done):** corporate burner/loaner programs for China/Russia travel, border device-search law and current high-risk-country posture, evil-maid and return-sanitization protocols, eSIM/faraday practice. So method claims are current and defensible.

## Recommended sequence (override freely)

- **Before July (launch):** **Coercive-ex / stalking** page (A-6) — highest-fit, clones the proven template, biggest underserved demand.
- **Right after launch:** **Extortion / leak** (A-7), then **Travel** (B-1) as the deliberate pilot of the new category + template.
- **Then evaluate:** if Travel converts, build out the rest of Category B (public role, wealth event, family-office retainer) where the recurring revenue is.
- **Hold:** Categories C & D as channels/stages, not pages, until the page system is proven wider.
