What your fee buys

Your protection day, step by step.

Not a product or a subscription — a full day of expert work, done in person and in your presence. This is what happens, in order.

  1. 01

    Threat model

    We map every device, account, and channel you rely on — and exactly who can reach each one.

  2. 02

    Indicators-of-compromise scan

    We check your devices and accounts for what may already be wrong: rogue sessions, unfamiliar logins, stalkerware, silent forwarding rules.

  3. 03

    Authentication hardening

    We end live sessions, rotate what's exposed, and set passkeys and your hardware key as the locks — so a password someone already knows is no longer enough.

  4. 04

    Operating-system hardening

    Encryption, lockdown settings, and OS configuration brought to the NSA, NIST, and NCSC baselines used by government agencies.

  5. 05

    App & cloud configuration

    Permissions, location sharing, family plans, shared albums, and backups reviewed one by one — and closed.

  6. 06

    Network, vehicle & connected devicesadd-ons & higher tiers

    Router, smart-home, and vehicle accounts brought back under your sole control.

  7. 07

    Coaching

    We train you on the habits, the hardware key, and the secure channel — so the protection holds long after the day ends.

  8. 08

    Certificate of Hardening

    A signed record of every measure applied, dated for your attorney and your legal file.

Questions
Which tier do I need?

Most individual cases are Personal Shield — one person, one phone, every account. Add devices, people, or your home from the add-ons. The Threat Assessment exists to settle this, and its fee comes off whichever tier you choose.

Can I add devices or people later?

Yes. Any add-on can be booked with your engagement or afterward, at the same fixed price.

What counts as my "primary device"?

Your phone — iOS or Android. It holds the most, so it's the heart of the engagement. A laptop, tablet, or PC is an additional device.

Are there any recurring fees?

No — each tier and add-on is one-time, except Annual Threat Monitoring and the Priority Retainer, which are optional yearly add-ons.

How do I pay, and is it private?

Discussed directly and confidentially when you book. Contact is by WhatsApp or Signal only — no forms, no third parties.
