## 1. What the service actually is

In-person, single-practitioner private cyber defense for people in high-stakes personal disputes. Dr. Lior Tabansky maps a client's full digital exposure and hardens every device, account and channel — in one day, in the client's presence. No remote access, no cloud, no logs. Ends with a signed Certificate of Hardening for the legal file. 1–2 clients a month.

## 2. The core marketing problem (Lior's framing — the spine of all copy)

**This is a new category. Nobody wakes up searching for it.** "Cyber" and "privacy" are too remote and generic to move someone — they sound like antivirus and cookie banners, not like the thing that just happened in their marriage. So the strategy is **translation**: we never sell "cybersecurity." We sell the resolution of a **life situation** the person is already living inside — a hostile divorce, a partner dispute, a contested inheritance.

**Consequence for IA:** the **situation pages are the real product** — they are simultaneously the landing pages (where intent-less traffic arrives via their lawyer, a friend, or a frightened late-night search) and the **persona definitions**. The homepage is the lobby; the situation pages are where conviction is built.

**The central tension to manage in copy:** depth vs. clarity. Each situation can be expanded with threat reasoning, external stories, and logic that builds belief — but every addition taxes concision and the white-glove calm. Resolving this tension page-by-page is the main editorial job (see §6).

## 3. Positioning statement

> For a person whose private life has become contested, **Personal Cyber Shield** is the one discreet expert who closes every digital door before it is used against them — in a single day, in person, and never spoken of again. Unlike security products and IT services, it is not a tool or a subscription; it is **Dr. Tabansky himself**, working the way a private physician or a master craftsman does.

- **Category we translate into:** not "cybersecurity" — "protection during [your situation]."
- **Reference feel:** Q — quiet mastery, bespoke instruments, unbothered by the technology.
- **Brand = the category; the Doctor = the rarity.** The phrase says *what*; the man is *why you*.

## 4. Audience

Primary: affluent Israelis in personal crisis (divorce, business split, inheritance) — and **their attorneys**, a major referral channel the current site ignores. Secondary: RU speakers (Israeli + international). The buyer is often acute, frightened, and time-pressed; the referrer (lawyer) is calm and evaluative. Copy must serve both registers.

## 5. Voice & message architecture

- **Voice:** "we," with Dr. Tabansky named at the trust moments. Contemporary-premium (HE authored first in spirit; EN built first). Calm, precise, zero marketing-speak, zero fear-pumping.
- **Always:** "your Personal Cyber Shield" / "the Shield." Never "a personal cyber shield."
- **Message hierarchy per page:** (1) name the situation in the client's own words; (2) make the hidden exposure concrete via a true vignette; (3) show the calm, finite resolution (one day, in person, certificate); (4) one quiet next step (Threat Assessment).
- **Proof carriers:** the true-case vignettes; the NSA/NIST/NCSC standards (EN/RU) vs. TAU + government-advisory weight (HE); the Certificate of Hardening; the 1–2-clients scarcity, stated once.

## 6a. Vignette standard (locked) — stories, not case notes

Vignettes are the proof engine and must be **written as scenes**: a person who was careful, an ordinary overlooked detail, and a quiet turn where it surfaces (in mediation, in court, in the filing). Specificity is the craft — "a street she'd lived on in 2009," "between a science poster and a birthday list," "a small blue dot." Calm register throughout; the story carries the fear, the frame never shouts. Each situation page = 2 restoried vignettes + an optional `[TODO-Lior]` third/external. **UPDATE 2026-06-10 (Lior):** the closing attribution line *"Based on a true case; details changed to protect those involved"* has been **removed site-wide** (all situations + homepages, EN/HE/RU); vignettes now stand on their own. See `Session-Handoff-2026-06-10.md`.

## 6b. The promise line (locked)

Canonical trust line, all pages/locales: **In person only · No remote access · No cloud · No logs · No app.** "No app" = nothing of ours ever lives on the client's device. Update project memory's promise wording accordingly.

## 7. Non-negotiables / guardrails

- Single prices only — never ranges. · Never the indefinite article for the brand. · No fear-pumping, no countdown-timer urgency. · No invented statistics or claims not already substantiated. · Defensive framing only — never imply offense against another person ("can you hack my partner back" → no). · In-person, no-logs, no-cloud promise stated plainly, not as a gimmick. · Contact only via WhatsApp/Signal.
