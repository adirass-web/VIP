Gate D3 · tokens v2.1 — coherence fix

Eight colors. One hue. Nothing else.

Your critique was right: v2's bg-2 sat at double the chroma of bg-0 (hence brown), and ink-dim drifted khaki. v2.1 locks every value to one hue axis — 36°, the gold's hue — at constant chroma per family: surfaces 8%, inks ≤7%. The whole site uses eight hex values; hairlines and washes are alphas of those eight, never new colors.
