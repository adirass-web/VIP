## 1. Pricing principles

1. **All displayed prices are VAT-inclusive** (Israeli VAT **18%**, 2026), grossed so the *net* equals the labour target. `net = displayed / 1.18`. No "+VAT" footnotes.
2. **Accessible anchor, premium ladder** — Personal is the low-friction entry; margin is recovered on Home, Inner Circle, and add-ons.
3. **Capacity is the real constraint (1-2 clients/month)** — price is the primary lever; the Threat Assessment is the qualifying front door.
4. **Value scales with the unit** — the person and the device. Tiers bound scope; add-ons meter overflow as honest, priced work.
5. **Defensive only. No fear-pumping.** Calm white-glove voice.

## 2. Value metric & unit

**The unit is the person and their primary device — the phone.** The phone is not a phone: it is the client's keys, wallet, archive, identity, location, and the second factor that authorises everything else. **Primary device = the client's phone (iOS/Android); a laptop, tablet, or PC is an additional device.** State plainly on the page; expand on the homepage.

## 3. The model — "Two Paths" from one anchor

```
                      SOVEREIGN  (both, fully — bespoke)
                        ^
        deeper +--------+--------+ wider
            HOME                INNER CIRCLE
        (one person,           (up to 3 people,
         whole environment)     each to Personal depth)
                        ^
                     PERSONAL  (anchor: one person, the phone)
                        ^
                  THREAT ASSESSMENT  (scoping census / front door)
```

The individual in a dispute wants *depth* (Home); the family/circle under threat wants *breadth* (Inner Circle). Present two arrows off the anchor, not one linear ladder.

## 5. Home Shield — the control-plane bound (scope-creep firewall)

Home is bounded by **method**, not device count. We do not harden 30 smart devices — we **segment and contain** them (NIST SP 800-213; CIS Controls v8). In scope: the **router** (firmware, admin, WPA3, DNS); **network segmentation** (Trusted / Guest / IoT, default-deny); the **accounts that command the home** (ISP, router, smart-home hubs) + MFA; **up to 2 additional personal devices** to the Personal standard. Out of scope -> add-on: per-device hardening beyond containment; devices beyond 2; the vehicle. The Threat Assessment census confirms Home scope before commitment.

## 8. Counter-surveillance — split

- **Digital counter-surveillance is in EVERY tier** — the step-02 IoC scan (stalkerware, hidden trackers, silent forwarding). State plainly per tier; it's the wedge.
- **Physical TSCM is Sovereign / by-arrangement only** — not a public add-on (a standalone "bug sweep" SKU is fear-pumpy and off-voice).

## 9. Unit economics (net basis, ~8h Personal day)

- Personal: net ₪11,864 / ~8h ~ **₪1,483/h** blended floor.
- Home marginal: net ₪16,102 over Personal for ~8-12h bounded work ~ **₪1,342-2,013/h**.
- Inner Circle: net ₪32,203 / 3 = **₪10,734/person**; vs 3x Personal (net ₪35,592) saves ~₪3,389 (**~9.5%**).
