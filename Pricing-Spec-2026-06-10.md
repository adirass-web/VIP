# Pricing Spec — 2026-06-10 (Model B: bounded tiers + à-la-carte add-ons)

Source of truth for /pricing. Single fixed prices only (no ranges).

## Value metric
The **device** (and the **person**). **Primary device = the client's phone (iOS/Android); a laptop, tablet, or PC is an additional device.** State plainly on the page.
Labour (Lior): phone ≈8h · extra device 3–7h · home network 3–4h · implied ≈₪1,900/h. Add-ons absorb variance into a flat price.

## Tiers (BOUNDED)
- Personal ₪15,000 — 1 person · phone · all accounts · 2 keys · certificate · coaching (~1 day)
- Extended ₪32,000 — Personal + home network + ONE additional device + ONE vehicle
- Inner Circle ₪38,000 — up to 3 people, each phone + accounts (no environment)
- Sovereign ₪95,000 — most comprehensive / bespoke · counter-surveillance · Monitoring + Retainer included

## Add-ons (any tier) — PROVISIONAL, Lior to confirm
- Additional device ₪7,500 · Home network ₪5,000 · Vehicle ₪4,000 · Additional person ₪10,000
- Counter-surveillance sweep — by arrangement · Annual Threat Monitoring ₪6,000/yr · Priority Retainer ₪12,000/yr

## Decisions
- Monitoring + Retainer = opt-in annual add-ons on ANY tier (bundled into Sovereign as a perk).
- Extended bounded (fixes scope creep). Inner Circle = people only. Sovereign people = by arrangement; ₪95,000 kept as anchor.
